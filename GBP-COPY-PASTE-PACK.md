## 13. Reviews (the single biggest ranking gap, per the audit)

This is where the listing is most exposed. Set up a simple loop:
- Text every finished-job customer your Google review link within 24 hours.
- Add a QR code to the final invoice or walkthrough sheet.
- Reply to every review within 24 hours, positive or negative.
- Never offer anything in exchange for a review (Google policy).

**Review request text (paste, then send):**
```
Hi [name], thanks for trusting Kayan Contracting with your roof. If you have 30 seconds, a quick Google review really helps our small local business. Here is the direct link: [your Google review link]. Thank you, Bryan.
```
To get your direct review link: in your GBP dashboard, click "Ask for reviews" and copy the short link it gives you.
