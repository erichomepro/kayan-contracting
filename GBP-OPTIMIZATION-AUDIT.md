## 3. GBP optimization recommendations (prioritized)

### Priority 1: Launch a review-generation system (highest ranking impact)
- Text every completed-job customer a **direct Google review link** within 24h of completion.
- Add a **QR code** to the final invoice/walkthrough sheet.
- 3-day follow-up text for non-responders.
- **Respond to every review within 24h** (positive and negative).
- Target: 2-3 new reviews/month, reaching 40+ in 6 months and 70+ in 12 months (to challenge Jayson Global).
- *Never incentivize* (Google policy violation).

### Priority 2: Categories and services
- **Primary:** Roofing Contractor
- **Secondary:** Roof Repair Service, Metal Roofing Contractor, Siding Contractor, Gutter Cleaning Service
- Add **all services individually** with descriptions: roof replacement, shingling, metal roofing, steel siding, roof repair, seamless eavestroughing, soffits & fascia, FLIR thermal inspection.
- Add **products** (Complete Roof Replacement, Metal Roofing Installation, Emergency Roof Repair).

### Priority 3: Business description (publish-ready draft)
> Kayan Contracting Ltd. has protected Parkland County homes for over 22 years. As an IKO Preferred Contractor with a BBB A+ rating, we handle roof replacement, shingling, metal roofing, steel siding, seamless eavestroughing, soffits & fascia, and roof repair across Stony Plain, Spruce Grove, Parkland County, Edmonton, and Lac Ste. Anne County. Every inspection includes free FLIR thermal imaging to catch hidden moisture and heat loss other roofers miss. 10-year workmanship warranty on all installations. 1,500+ homeowners served. Call (780) 984-0221 for a free estimate.

### Priority 4: Photos and media
- Upload 50+ real project photos (Kayan already has 100+ on site, a major advantage over stock-photo competitors).
- Categorize: exterior, before/after, team, metal, steel siding, thermal imaging reveals.
- Feature **FLIR thermal images**. No competitor markets this.

### Priority 5: NAP consistency and citations
- Verify identical Name/Address/Phone across GBP, BBB, Facebook, TrustedPros, HomeStars, Yelp, Apple Maps, Bing Places, Canada411.
- Current public listings show consistent NAP; lock it before scaling citations.

### Priority 6: Weekly GBP posts
- One post/week (offers, project spotlights, seasonal tips). An active GBP is a ranking signal and most local competitors neglect it.
